THE TOP 3 DESTINATIONS IN THE PHILIPPINES


The Philippines is an amazing country with more than 7,000 islands, each offering a variety of attractions and experiences.

These are the top 3 destinations in the Philippines:


Boracay

Boracay is famous for its white sand beaches, water activities, and nightlife. It has more than 12 beaches to choose from, each with its own charm and character. Some of the best beaches are White Beach, Puka Shell Beach, and Bulabog Beach

Manila

Manila is the capital city of the Philippines and the center of its economic and cultural activities. It has a rich history and heritage, as well as modern amenities and entertainment. Some of the top attractions in Manila are Fort Santiago, Manila Ocean Park, and Rizal Park

Bohol

Bohol is an island that showcases the natural beauty and diversity of the Philippines. It is home to the Chocolate Hills, a UNESCO-protected site with more than 1,000 cone-shaped hills that turn brown in the dry season. It is also where you can find the tarsier sanctuary, where you can see the tiny and adorable primates up close. Bohol also has some of the best beaches in the country, such as Panglao Island and Alona Beach
