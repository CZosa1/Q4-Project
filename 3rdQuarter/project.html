Aliensrock 


Aliensrock, also known as Tyler, is an American YouTube and Twitch content creator with 620,000+ subscribers that uploads videos or livestreams daily. His content is mostly focused on video games, especially the puzzle genre.

Tyler is passionate about puzzle games, and his addiction to them is evident in his daily uploads. Aliensrock's channel is a treasure trove for puzzle lovers, whether it be designing and executing reversal puzzles, achieving great scores or discovering hidden gems.
